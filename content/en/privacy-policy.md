### Tracking Tech & Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and squirrel away certain information. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts, which help us gather and track info, plus improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we might use include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file that's placed on your Device. You can tell your browser to give all Cookies the boot or to give you a heads-up when a Cookie is being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use certain awesome parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say "no thanks" to Cookies, our Service might just use them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some sections of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These bad boys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out how popular a certain section is and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies hang around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital to give you services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to deliver those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have given their thumbs-up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are the key to us remembering the choices you make when you use the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personal experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

Want more info on the Cookies we use and your options regarding them? Just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
